About & Market Analysis

Complete venture analysis — Electro Spark (VEN-ELEC-5613) · Niralthiruvizha 3.0 · SRIT Coimbatore

🎯

Problem

People in rural India struggle to access and understand basic legal information due to language barriers and complex legal procedures. 70% of Indians lack legal aid access.

💡

Solution

AI-powered multilingual legal chatbot accessible via mobile app and WhatsApp, answering legal queries in regional Indian languages through text and voice input — free for all.

📊

Market Size

Indian Legal Tech Market: USD 1.7B (2023) growing at 27.4% CAGR (2023–2030). Source: Inc42 2023, Fortune Business Insights 2024.

✨ Key Features

🌐

10+ Regional Languages

Tamil, Hindi, Telugu, Kannada, Malayalam, Marathi, Bengali, Gujarati + more. Auto Unicode-based language detection.

🎤

Voice Input

Voice-based queries using Web Speech API — critical for low-literacy rural users. Supports all major Indian regional languages in Chrome/Edge.

📱

WhatsApp Integration (Backend)

WhatsApp Business API ready — accessible on a platform 500M+ Indians already use daily. No app download needed.

📶

Offline FAQ Mode

Core legal Q&A works without internet using local JS database. Designed for rural areas with low connectivity.

💳

Subscription Model

Free tier (20 queries/day) → Premium (₹99/mo, unlimited) → NGO Plan (₹999/mo, multi-user API). Drives 3-year financial roadmap to profitability.
